📖 How do I use the Glossary?

The BTL Glossary explains all key statistical terms from the tools of the Bayes Thinking Lab and the MethodsLab.

  • Search – Type a term or keyword into the search field. Cards are filtered live.
  • Category – Click a category button to see only terms from that area (e.g. Bayes only).
  • A–Z – Jump directly to the first term starting with the selected letter.

Each entry contains: a Definition, an Intuition (what does the term mean in practice?) and Related terms.
